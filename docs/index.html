Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't.

📦
Share with .mcpack files

Bundle your entire modpack into one shareable file. Send it to friends and they can install everything in seconds.

⚡
Automatic mod downloads

Mods are fetched directly from Modrinth. No manual downloads, no hunting for the right version.

🔒
Isolated installations

Each modpack gets its own fully separate Minecraft installation — no conflicts with your other profiles ever.

🔧
Built-in modpack builder

Create modpacks from scratch. Search for mods by Modrinth ID, add a thumbnail and description, and export to share.

🔄
Easy reinstalls

Update a modpack and reinstall with one click. Your worlds, settings, and saves are always kept safe.

🪄
All major loaders

Supports Fabric, Forge, NeoForge, Quilt, and Vanilla out of the box. Loader installers are handled automatically.

How it works

Up and running
in minutes.

1
Download & install Simple Mod Installer

Grab the installer below and run it. It works alongside your existing Minecraft launcher — nothing is replaced or changed.

2
Import or create a modpack

Open a .mcpack file from a friend, or use the built-in builder to create your own by searching Modrinth for mods.

3
Click install

The app downloads Minecraft, installs the mod loader, and fetches every mod automatically. A profile appears in your Minecraft launcher ready to play.

4
Open Minecraft and play

Launch the Minecraft launcher, select your new profile, and you're in. Share the .mcpack file with friends so they can do the same.
